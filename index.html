The Studio

A quiet pursuit of elegance, restraint, and craft.

Atelier Noir is an interior design studio founded on the belief that great spaces are composed in silence — through proportion, light, and considered material choices.

From private residences to boutique hospitality, our work is grounded in collaboration with architects, artisans, and clients who value the beauty of doing fewer things, exceptionally well.

— Keith Tan, Founder
